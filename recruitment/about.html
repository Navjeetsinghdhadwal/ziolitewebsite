About us

Ziolite Recruitment was started as a result of a gap in the market for an agency that put’s Service as its number one priority. To us the essence of Recruitment is to provide a Service to companies and individuals and to help both parties make that perfect match. The result of this is happy Employers and Employees.

We continue to strive to deliver the highest quality of service possible. This is made possible by our employees focusing on Service with the end result being happier clients and a stronger working relationship with our clients.

Our Aim

We are working towards being recognised as one of India’s Leading Specialist IT Recruitment Agencies with a focus on Service and Delivery. Since the company was founded we have experienced continuous growth and support from all sizes of organisations ranging from Start-ups to Large Multi-Nationals.

Our growth is dictated by the time and training we provide to each member of staff in order to ensure they can provide the highest level of service and advice in order to add real value to the recruitment process.

We will continue to grow as a company as long as our fundamentals can be managed and maintained.
