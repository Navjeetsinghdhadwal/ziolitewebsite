About

Ziolite is a process-oriented consulting and services company assisting organizations with their HR , Para-legal , Real Estate, Venture Capital needs by providing recruitment & other related services.We are simplifying the whole recruitment process and other legal as well as finanial service by giving technology-based tools and tailored services to large corporate. We seamlessly connect the right candidate with the right client, demonstrating, passion, integrity, professionalism and transparency at all times.Started and conceptualized by dynamic and visionary entrepreneurs, Ziolite was established with a purpose of being the best in the business.
